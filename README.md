🍽️ Swiggy Sales & Customer Behavior Dashboard 📊

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Swiggy's sales performance and customer ordering behavior across India. It provides business-critical insights into revenue trends, customer satisfaction, food preferences, regional performance, and restaurant performance. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs and make data-driven business decisions through interactive visualizations.

🚀 Key Features
💰 ₹51.58M Total Sales generated from 192.25K customer orders

⭐ 4.34 Average Customer Rating based on 5.39M customer ratings

💳 ₹268.32 Average Order Value, highlighting customer spending patterns

🥗 Veg food contributes approximately 65% of total sales, while Non-Veg contributes 35%

📍 Maharashtra is the highest revenue-generating state

🏙️ Bengaluru is the top-performing city by sales

📈 Interactive Monthly, Daily, Weekly, and Quarterly sales analysis

🎛️ Dashboard is fully interactive with Month, State, and Restaurant slicers
